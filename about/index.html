About Me

Hi, I'm Anton Van Assche, a Linux System & Automation Engineer at SFPD (Federale Pensioendienst - Service fédéral des pensions).

I spend my days automating IT infrastructures, from managing Red Hat Linux virtual machines to OpenShift clusters. My go-to tools are RHEL, OpenShift, Ansible, Puppet, Bash, and Python.

I love making complex systems reliable and repeatable, scripting my way out of repetitive tasks, and tinkering with my homelab or Neovim plugins for fun (I use Neovim btw!). Sometimes, when no existing tool fits the bill, I build my own overly engineered CLI utilities, looking at you, sw.

Outside of tech, I'm a long-time épée fencer and a music enthusiast, ranging through all kinds of genres.

Some Projects

  • music-controls.nvim - Neovim plugin to control music players.
  • date-time-inserter.nvim - Neovim plugin to insert date and time.
  • md-headers.nvim - Neovim plugin to easily navigate Markdown headers.
  • puppetcode - Puppetcode for my homelab.
  • sw - An overly complicated wallpaper switcher for Wayland.
